Capstone Project

- myDent Dental Web Enhancement

Description: The initiative to develop a website for the myDent dental office is designed to bolster the clinic's digital footprint, optimize patient engagement, and drive a significant uptick in appointment bookings. By integrating features such as responsive design, comprehensive service information, online booking capabilities, patient testimonials, and a resourceful blog, the project aims to enhance the online user experience for both existing and prospective patients. Furthermore, it seeks to simplify internal operations by incorporating tools for managing staff schedules and inventory, underscoring a holistic approach to digital transformation.
